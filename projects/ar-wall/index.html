AR Wall

JP Carrascal (2018)
An augmented reality customer wall for UX research results at Microsoft
AR Wall is an innovative augmented reality installation designed to share results from a UX research project conducted at Microsoft. This interactive wall transforms traditional presentation methods by using AR technology to create an immersive and engaging way to communicate research findings and insights.

The project leverages augmented reality to overlay digital information onto a physical wall space, allowing viewers to interact with research data, visualizations, and findings in a spatial and intuitive manner. This approach makes complex UX research results more accessible and engaging for diverse audiences within the organization.

AR Wall represents an innovative application of emerging technologies in corporate environments, demonstrating how AR can be used to enhance knowledge sharing and data presentation in professional settings. The project bridges the gap between traditional research communication and modern interactive technologies.

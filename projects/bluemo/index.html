BlueMO

JP Carrascal (2016)
Bluetooth LE to MIDI/OSC translator for Mac OS X
BlueMO is a Bluetooth Low Energy to MIDI/OSC translator application designed specifically for Mac OS X. This utility application enables seamless communication between Bluetooth LE devices and music software, allowing wireless controllers and sensors to interface with digital audio workstations and other music applications.

The application serves as a bridge between the growing ecosystem of Bluetooth LE devices and traditional music production software, translating wireless sensor data into MIDI messages or OSC (Open Sound Control) commands that can be understood by music software. This opens up new possibilities for wireless musical controllers and interactive performance systems.

BlueMO represents an important tool for musicians, performers, and developers working with wireless music technology, providing a reliable and efficient way to integrate Bluetooth LE devices into existing music production workflows.

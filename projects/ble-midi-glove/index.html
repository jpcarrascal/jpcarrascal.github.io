BLE-MIDI Glove Controller

JP Carrascal (2021)
A Bluetooth LE-MIDI-based glove controller for musical performance
The BLE-MIDI Glove Controller is an innovative wearable musical interface that combines Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology with MIDI communication protocols. This project represents a sophisticated approach to gestural music control, allowing performers to manipulate sound through natural hand movements and finger gestures.

Built using modern wireless technology, the glove controller offers freedom of movement while maintaining reliable, low-latency communication with digital audio workstations and synthesizers. The device incorporates multiple sensors to capture various aspects of hand motion and finger positioning, translating these into expressive musical parameters.

This project contributes to the growing field of embodied musical interfaces, offering musicians new ways to express themselves through technology while maintaining the intuitive connection between physical gesture and musical expression.

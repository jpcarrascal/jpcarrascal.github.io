A Sense Of Surrounding

Tom DeMajo, JP Carrascal (2024)
Participatory soundscape to promote environmental awareness. Presented at Ars Electronica 2024 (Linz, Austria). Supported by ArtEO / www.arteo.earth
A Sense Of Surrounding is a participatory soundscape installation designed to promote environmental awareness through interactive audio experiences. This project explores how sound can be used as a medium to connect people with their environmental surroundings and foster a deeper understanding of ecological relationships.

The installation was presented at Ars Electronica 2024 in Linz, Austria, one of the world's most prestigious festivals for art, technology, and society. The project was supported by ArtEO (www.arteo.earth), an organization dedicated to promoting environmental consciousness through artistic expression.

Through participatory interaction, visitors become part of the soundscape, creating an immersive experience that highlights the interconnectedness between human activity and environmental impact.

Exposition Aperture

Tom DeMajo, JP Carrascal (2024)
Large format digital video presented at Ars Electronica 2024 (Linz, Austria). Supported by ArtEO.
Exposition Aperture begins with a single pixel-wide line, which gradually opens like a curtain to reveal complete satellite images, accompanied by an unsettling and shifting soundscape. This is a collaboration with Tom DeMajo, who developed the video based on a soundtrack generated with SynthEO (included in this portfolio).

Exhibition:

Ars Electronica 2024, Linz, Austria

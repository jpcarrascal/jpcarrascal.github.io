Selected Creative Technology Projects

Exploring participation, immersion, and emotion through interactive systems

I design and build interactive systems where technology is used not just for utility, but for expression, emotion, and storytelling.

My work spans web-based interactive platforms, immersive experiences, and participatory installations, often combining real-time systems, sound, and physical interaction. I am interested in how technology can create shared experiences by inviting participation, shaping attention, and fostering emotional connection in public and collective contexts.
